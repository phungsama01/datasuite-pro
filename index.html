// FAQ

Common questions answered.

Is my data safe? Does DataSuite Pro upload my files anywhere? +
No. Every file operation — transformation, cleaning, merging, mapping — runs entirely inside your browser using local JavaScript. Your files are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored anywhere outside your own machine. This is by design and is a core architectural commitment, not just a policy.
How does the free tier work exactly? +
You get 3 full free exports — no account, no credit card required. Process real files with real data. See actual output. Only after your 3rd free export will you be prompted to upgrade. We believe you should experience results before spending money.
When does my subscription activate after payment? +
Instantly. Payment is handled by Paddle, our billing partner. The moment your payment is confirmed by Paddle, your plan unlocks without any manual approval step. No waiting, no emails to send, no team to contact.
What file formats are supported? +
The platform supports CSV, JSON, TSV, XLSX (Excel), and XML as both input and output formats depending on the module. The Transformer module handles cross-format conversions. The Merger and Cleaner modules work with CSV and Excel by default.
Is there a row limit on the free plan? +
The free tier gives you 3 complete exports with no row restriction — process files of any size during your free trial. After your 3 free exports are used, row processing is unlimited on the Enterprise Pro plan, and up to 10,000 rows per operation on the Starter Suite.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? +
Yes, with no penalty or notice period. You can cancel anytime via Paddle's customer portal or by emailing datasuitepro.help@gmail.com. Your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period.
