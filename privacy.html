DataSuitePro Data Architecture

Privacy Framework

Last revised: 2026-06-06 · Zero-Retention Engine

DataSuitePro is engineered with a strict zero-retention privacy architecture. This document describes how client files, database matrices, and processing logic are handled entirely within your local environment. The platform is operated under the sole proprietorship of Preetam Dubey, Waridasharathpur, Gonda, Uttar Pradesh - 221304. Support inquiries: datasuitepro.help@gmail.com.

1. Core Privacy Principle: Local-First Execution

NO FILE UPLOAD · NO SERVER-SIDE STORAGE All client files, CSV mappings, column schemas, and database matrices are processed strictly inside your local web browser memory using localized JavaScript arrays. DataSuitePro does not transmit, upload, or replicate your raw datasets to any external server, cloud bucket, or third-party storage layer. The processing engine operates entirely within the client-side runtime.

When you import a CSV, Excel file, or connect a tabular dataset, the content is read by the browser's FileReader API and parsed into ephemeral JavaScript objects. These objects exist only for the duration of your browser session or until you navigate away. No backend ingestion endpoint exists.

2. What Leaves Your Browser (Minimal License Telemetry)

The only external communication involves license validation calls to our Merchant of Record (Paddle) to verify subscription status. These calls contain a license token and tier identifier—never your file contents, row data, or column headers. The trial counter stored in localStorage remains entirely client-side and is not synchronized with any remote database.

BROWSER STORAGE ARTIFACTS Keys used: dspro_trial_cycles, dspro_license_cache. No analytics SDKs, no tracking pixels, no behavioral profiling scripts are embedded.

3. Enterprise Pro Suite & Unlimited Ingestion

Even under the Enterprise Pro Suite Node ($299/month or $2,988/year) with absolute unlimited file ingestion arrays, the zero-retention architecture remains inviolable. Larger datasets are processed using chunked streaming within the browser's memory heap; at no point are chunks offloaded to a remote server. The "unlimited" designation refers to row/volume constraints being lifted, not a shift to cloud processing.

4. No Personal Data Collection

DataSuitePro does not require account creation that collects personal details beyond the email address provided to Paddle for payment receipts. We do not store, sell, or share any user data. The support inbox (datasuitepro.help@gmail.com) is used exclusively for correspondence and is never linked to in-browser processing data.

5. Third-Party Services & Merchant Processing

Payment processing is handled entirely by Paddle. Their privacy policy governs the collection of billing details. DataSuitePro never sees or stores your full credit card number. The operational entity Preetam Dubey receives only transaction confirmation and license metadata necessary to provision your subscription tier.

6. Changes & Jurisdiction

This privacy framework may be updated to reflect technical enhancements. Continued use of the platform implies acceptance. Any legal interpretation shall fall under the jurisdiction of courts in Uttar Pradesh, India, consistent with the Terms of Service.
