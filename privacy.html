Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 4, 2026

1. Pure Client-Side Processing Blueprint

DataSuite Pro operates on a zero-server architecture. All spreadsheets, JSON files, and databases processed via our tools are computed strictly within your local browser's memory arrays. No file data is ever uploaded, transmitted, or stored on any server owned by DataSuite Pro or third parties.

2. Information We Collect

Because processing happens client-side, we have no access to your data records. We only collect standard account management data via our Merchant of Record, Paddle, including your email address, payment details, and regional billing address required for international tax execution.

3. Compliance Structure (GDPR & SOC2 Readiness)

Our localized execution model ensures that your data processing complies naturally with GDPR, HIPAA, and corporate data protection guidelines, as sensitive financial or medical records never leave your local physical machine during transformation.

4. Contact Information

For operations, corporate requests, or verification, contact our global operations unit managed in partnership with our corporate billing unit in India.
